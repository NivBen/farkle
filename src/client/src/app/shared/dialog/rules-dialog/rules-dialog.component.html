Farkle Rules



Objective of the game


gain points and reach the goal before other players!


  • Whoever starts is randomly determined
  • Each player plays starts his turn with all 6 dice.
  • Player rolls them in order to make combinations to obtain points.

1 and 5 are the only numbers that can be picked up as points from as individuals (1 die) or pairs (2 similar dice)

Straight:

arrow_right_alt = 500p
arrow_right_alt = 750p
arrow_right_alt = 1500p

Anything other than the plays listed above is considered an "illegal" move and cannot be "confirmed" by the player.

Bust:
If at any point in your turn you roll a throw with no available "legal" moves you lose all the points you accumulated during your current turn and the turn will proceed to the next player

Joker:
One die out of the 6 is a joker die - the value 1 on that die is replaced by the joker, other values on that die behaves the same as the others.
if you get a joker - it will automatically change to a number that will maximize your point output (it will also change in order to "legalize" the move)

When you "confirm" a throw with a die/dice you gain the points they are worth and not throw them again for that turn, now you can choose to either roll another throw with the remaining dice you have or to stop your turn and bank the points you gathered in this turn and the turn will proceed to the next player

If you are able to pickup all the dice during your turn you can choose to continue throwing with all the 6 dice becoming available again (essentially starting another fresh turn with all the points you gathered up to now) - Beware that you may still get a bust at any point during your turn!
